Sai Meer Degree College, Chhibramau Kannauj

The purpose of the establishment of the college is to make proper arrangements for higher education for the boys and girls of rural areas. While there are many inter-colleges in rural areas for the education of boys and girls, there is no college with state-of-the-art facilities for higher education, due to which most of the students remain deprived of higher education.
